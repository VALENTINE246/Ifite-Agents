Lodge Agent Regulations

  1. All lodge agents must be registered with the Ifite community council
  2. Registration must be renewed annually
  3. Agents must maintain proper records of all lodgers
  4. All lodgers must be properly identified before accommodation
  5. Agents must report suspicious activities to local authorities
  6. Minimum standards of accommodation must be maintained
  7. No agent shall charge above approved rates
  8. Agents must participate in community security meetings
  9. Proper sanitation must be maintained at all times
  10. Violations may result in fines or revocation of registration
